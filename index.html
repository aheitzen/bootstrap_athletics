Fun in the Sun: Sun Exposure and your Health

Welcome back to Horizon Health! Today I want to shed some light on sun exposure and how we can enjoy the sun and protect our health simultaneously. All of our athletes are drawn to water in some way or another, and water and sunshine go together like ice cream and pie.
